About Us

Welcome to Az Speeedy Car & Fleet Details, your trusted partner for top-notch car detailing and fleet services. With a dedicated team of professionals and a wide range of offerings, we are committed to providing exceptional care for your vehicles. From basic Vedic washes to full details and pressure washing services for your sidewalks and driveways, we offer comprehensive solutions to meet all your automotive needs.

Key Services

  • Car Detailing

    Expert care for your vehicle's appearance. Hand wash, wax, interior cleaning, and paint correction.

  • Fleet Detailing

    Specialized services for work truck and golf car fleets. Maintain a pristine fleet image.

  • Pressure Washing

    We offer professional pressure washing services to keep your exteriors spotless and well-maintained.
